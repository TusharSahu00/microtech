Overall, degree-based courses are an excellent choice for students looking to gain a comprehensive education in a specific field of study.

Job
Oriented Courses

Job-oriented courses are training programs that are specifically designed to prepare individuals for a specific occupation or career. These courses may include technical skills training, on-the-job experience, and certifications that are necessary for a particular job. Examples of job-oriented courses include programming, RSCIT, TAllY and DCA.
They are often shorter in duration than degree-oriented courses and can provide students with a faster route to employment. Many of them can be completed in 1 to 2 years and are focused on providing hands-on training and practical experience that is directly relevant to the job market.
